  1. "Intro Clip" which performs a climb jump clip near spawn, jumping out of bounds through the ceiling
  2. Backwards long jump over the first cutscene trigger
  3. Mozzdor jumps across the void to bypass the glowing MNF sword cutscene trigger
  4. Climb jumps along the side of the long statue room to bypass the Zelda nerd cutscenes
  5. Mozzdor jumps across the void to reach the exit trigger
